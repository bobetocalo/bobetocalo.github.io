Biography

I am currently a PhD student at the Technical University of Madrid in the PCR Computer Vision Lab under the supervision of Luis Baumela and José Miguel Buenaposada. My current interests are in facial analysis problems such as face detection, head-pose estimation, landmarks features localization and visual attributes description.
