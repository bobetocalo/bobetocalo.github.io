Computer Vision Engineer

Telefónica

Biography

Dr. Roberto Valle has a role as Computer Vision Engineer at Telefónica since 2020. He focused on projects that use telco data and satellite imagery to understand the management of natural disasters, pollution in cities, social indicators and epidemics (e.g. COVID-19), among others. During his prior studies, he specializes in deep learning and machine learning. He holds a PhD in Artificial Intelligence at PCR Computer Vision Lab, where he has researched an interesting case of study that concerns facial analysis problems such as face detection, head-pose estimation, landmarks features localization and visual attributes description. He has been also an EIT Doctoral School student, where he acquired a mindset for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (I&E), complemented with an internship at the Computer Vision Laboratory under the supervision of Prof. Georgios Tzimiropoulos.
